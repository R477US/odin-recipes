Vegetarian Tacos Recipe

Introduction

A taco is a traditional Mexican dish consisting of a small hand sized tortilla topped with a filling. The tortilla is folded around the filling and eaten by hand.

For this recipe the fillings will be vegetarian. When serving lay out of your favorite toppings so everyone can make their own perfect taco. Tacos are an excellent meal to be eaten all-year round but best served on a Tuesday.

Ingredients

  • 1 pack of corn or flour tortillas

    • Fillings

  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 onion diced
  • 1 tomato diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 block of tofu, shredded
  • 1 block of cheese, shredded
  • 15oz corn, 1 can
  • 19oz of black beans, 1 can
  • 1 tbsp of soya sauce
  • salt and pepper, to taste

    • Sauce

  • 4 Tbsp of plain yogurt
  • 4 Tbsp of mayonnaise
  • 2 tbsp of lemon juice
  • 1 tsp of cumin
  • Parsley

Steps

  1. Pour vegetable oil into a hot pan with the shredded tofu and soya sauce. Cook until tofu has cripsy exterior or until desired consistency.
  2. In a a separate pan heat a tortilla with shredded cheese on top until the cheese melts. Once cooked set aside.
  3. For the sauce, add mayonnaise, plain yogurt, lemon juice, cumin and parsley to a food processor then mix for about 30 seconds.
  4. Assemble tacos by adding the rest of the rest of the ingredients and pouring the salsa verde sauce on top of it all. Serve and enjoy
